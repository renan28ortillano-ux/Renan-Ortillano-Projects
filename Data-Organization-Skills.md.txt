# 🧹 Data Cleaning & Organization Experience
 ## Overview
 In my work, I often encountered raw data that was unorganized, had duplicates, incorrect formats, and inconsistencies. My role was to fix these issues to make the data reliable and usable.
 ## 🛠️ Steps I Took:
 *1. Identifying Errors*
 *   Checked data carefully to spot mistakes, wrong spellings, and wrong formats.
 *2. Removing Duplicates*
 *   Used Excel tools and functions to find and delete repeated entries to ensure uniqueness.
 *3. Standardizing Data*
 *   Made sure all dates, names, codes, and values follow the same format and structure.
 *   Example: Converting all dates to "YYYY-MM-DD" format.
 *4. Data Validation*
 *   Applied rules to prevent wrong data from being entered.
 *   Ensured consistency especially in classification and labeling tasks.
 *5. Sorting and Filtering*
 *   Arranged data logically so it is easy to read, understand, and analyze.
 ## 🎯 Result
 After the process, the data became *accurate, clean, consistent, and ready for analysis or reporting.*
 ## 💡 Key Skills Applied
 *   High Attention to Detail
 *   Accuracy & Precision
 *   Proficiency in Microsoft Excel (Data Tools, Formulas, Formatting)
 *   Quality Control
